Erin Gruwell

What makes a hero? A hero is someone who inspires you to push yourself. A hero is someone who is willing to work harder to help others.

Erin Gruwell was a teacher at Wilson highschool. She created a safe haven for so many students at her school.She broke social normals in her class room and reunited people who had seen eachother as enemies before. Her class became a family. She inspired her students to write and express themselves. Mrs. G was someone her pushed her students to dream bigger. Gruwell was a teacher that new she had a challenge a head of her but worked three jobs and did everything in her power to teach her students there was more to this work than gang wars and trouble.

I challenge you to watch the movie Freedom Writers on Netflix and read The Freedom Writers Diary: How a Teacher and 150 Teens Used Writing to Change Themselves and the World Around Them. I challenge you to think about others, their situations and take that into effect when judging a person. We dont always know what is going on in someones life until you really get to know them.
